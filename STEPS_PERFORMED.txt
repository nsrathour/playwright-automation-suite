COMMON INTERVIEW QUESTIONS:
------------------------------

Q: Why Playwright over Selenium?
A: Playwright is faster, has better auto-wait mechanisms, supports modern
   web features, and has built-in support for multiple browsers without
   additional drivers.

Q: How do you handle waits?
A: Playwright has smart auto-waiting built-in. It waits for elements to be
   actionable before performing actions. For custom waits, I use
   waitForSelector() or waitForLoadState().

Q: How do you handle flaky tests?
A: I use proper selectors (data-testid), implement retries in config,
   ensure proper waits, and keep tests independent.

Q: What's your test structure?
A: I follow the Arrange-Act-Assert pattern. Setup test data, perform
   actions, then verify expected outcomes.
